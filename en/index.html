AI醫療新局 臺灣鏈結全球

2026 Global Summit on
AI-Enabled Healthcare Forum

As AI Healthcare Enters the Era of Commercialization,
How Can Taiwan Expand Globally?

AI is rapidly transforming global healthcare, from clinical applications to market expansion, making smart healthcare a major driver of health technology growth.

The 2026 Global Summit on AI-Enabled Healthcare Strategy brings together international medical, technology, and industry leaders to discuss AI healthcare commercialization, clinical validation, data applications, and global market opportunities.

The summit aims to explore practical strategies for connecting Taiwan’s smart healthcare strengths with international markets and creating new industry value.

Highlights

Global

Global AI Healthcare Trends

Experts from Massachusetts General Hospital and GE HealthCare discuss global AI healthcare adoption, clinical validation, and commercialization.

Local

Taiwan Health Tech Opportunitiesp

Explore how Taiwan can leverage healthcare data, AI, and clinical strengths to build global competitiveness.

Market

Pathways to Global Markets

Learn how AI healthcare companies can expand internationally through validation, regulatory planning, and market access.
